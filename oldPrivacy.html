PRIVACY POLICY FOR WorkRecords

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application App Name WorkRecords for mobile devices that was created by Mohammad Shehab. WorkRecords is a tool to keep track of your work records such as the times that you took a vacation or got sick or couldn't go to work because of an emergency or the amount of permissions you took in a speceific date and hopefully many more features in the future. 

 

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

The Application obtains the information you provide when you use the app and its kept on your device and/or iCloud if enabled. Neither We nor anyone except you have access to these information. 

 

When you use the Application, you generally provide your total permission hours, total sick days, total emergency days, and total vacation days;

Nothing is collected automatically. If any were to be collected, it would be kept on the device
